ABOUT US

France

When we returned home, we couldn't stop thinking about those wonderful flower shops. That's when the idea of opening our own flower store was born. We wanted to create a place where people could find beautiful flowers and experience the same sense of joy and inspiration that we felt during our travels.

The beginning wasn't easy. We had to find the right location, purchase shelves and flower stands, decorate the store, and learn how to manage every detail of the business. There were many challenges along the way, but our passion for flowers kept us moving forward.

Our story began with a trip to France.

During our journey, my friend and I fell in love with the charming flower boutiques that could be found on almost every corner. Their elegant arrangements, beautiful colors, and unique atmosphere inspired us deeply.

As time passed, our small shop began to grow. More customers discovered us, trusted us with their special moments, and supported our dream. We expanded our collection, improved our services, and continued creating beautiful floral arrangements for every occasion.

Today, we are proud to take the next step in our journey by launching this website. Here, you can explore our flowers, place orders online, contact our team, and learn more about who we are and how our story began.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We look forward to helping you
life's special moments with flowers.
